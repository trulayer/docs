The TruLayer TypeScript SDK works alongside the [Vercel AI SDK](https://sdk.vercel.ai) for streaming responses and tool-calling agents. Wrap your `streamText` or `generateText` calls in a TruLayer trace to capture prompts, completions, token usage, and tool invocations automatically.

See the [TypeScript SDK tutorial](/sdks/typescript/tutorial) for the current integration pattern.
